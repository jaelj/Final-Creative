What is Coronavirus and Why Should I Care?

Coronavirus (COVID-19):

A virus strain that comes from the large family of coronaviruses. It is transmitted from animals to people and can cause a multitude of problems ranging from cold-like to severe flu-like symptoms. This virus spreads rapidly and can stay in the air or on a surface for days.

So What, Why Should I Care?

This virus is very contagious and could put you or your loves ones at risk of dying. Several people who have it are a-symptomatic but can still be a carrier.
